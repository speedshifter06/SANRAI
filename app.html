About SANRAI

The Problem

We are tired of seeing brilliant developers burn out over administrative tasks. You shouldn't have to feel a knot of anxiety every morning at 9:55 AM, frantically figuring out what to say in your 10:00 AM Scrum call.

The Solution

SANRAI translates your chaotic, unstructured, deeply technical workday into the rigid, polished reporting formats your management demands. You just dump your thoughts. We do the corporate translation.

Privacy Policy

100% Local. Zero Backend.

Your work notes are your intellectual property. SANRAI operates purely as a "Local-First" application. Everything is stored in your browser's LocalStorage. We possess zero backend databases. We cannot see, sell, or analyze your data.

Bring Your Own Key (BYOK)

Data goes straight from your browser to Google's Gemini API. We act only as the interface. Clearing your browser cache permanently destroys all your SANRAI data.

Terms & Conditions

Your Responsibility

SANRAI is a productivity tool. It is your strict responsibility to verify the accuracy of AI-generated stand-ups before submission. Do not paste highly sensitive or classified company secrets into the API.

No Liability

Due to the local-first nature, we cannot recover lost data if you clear your browser. Use the Export features regularly.
