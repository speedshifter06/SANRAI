About SANRAI

The Administrative Trap

We are tired of seeing brilliant engineers burn out over administrative tasks. You didn't spend years mastering complex algorithms and system architectures just to feel a knot of anxiety every morning at 9:55 AM, frantically searching your git commits to figure out what to say in your 10:00 AM Scrum call.

You shouldn't have to sacrifice your Friday evenings, draining your remaining mental energy to remember what you accomplished on Tuesday, just to fill out a soulless corporate spreadsheet that no one actually reads.

Why We Exist

Indian IT professionals are drowning in context-switching. Between coding, testing, client calls, and endless Slack messages, the actual "work" gets lost in the noise of "reporting the work." This constant shift kills productivity and drains emotional energy.

SANRAI was born out of a simple necessity: to give developers their time and focus back. We act as the ultimate buffer—the translator between your chaotic, unstructured, deeply technical workday, and the rigid, polished reporting formats your management demands.

Our Vision

We envision a future where developers never have to write another timesheet manually. A future where you just talk or dump your thoughts into our engine, and the AI handles the corporate paperwork. Code more. Manage less.

Privacy Policy

1. Absolute Data Ownership

At SANRAI, we fundamentally believe that your work, your codebase, and your daily notes are your intellectual property. We have designed our architecture to reflect this belief. SANRAI operates purely as a "Local-First" application. This means your workflow data, timesheets, and daily logs are stored strictly within your own browser's LocalStorage. We possess zero backend databases. We cannot see, sell, or analyze your data because we simply do not have it.

2. The BYOK (Bring Your Own Key) Model

To ensure 100% corporate compliance and bypass stringent IT firewall restrictions, your data is never routed through our servers. When you request AI structuring, your data is processed directly between your browser and the Google Gemini API using your personal or company-provided API key. SANRAI acts merely as the interface, never as a middleman.

3. Data Lifecycle & The Right to be Forgotten

You have absolute manual control over your data footprint. You can edit, modify, or delete any generated report instantly. Items you choose to delete are moved to a local Recycle Bin to prevent accidental loss, where they are securely held for 90 days before being permanently and irrevocably purged. Furthermore, simply clearing your browser's cache immediately destroys all SANRAI data associated with that browser.

4. Telemetry and Tracking

We do not use invasive third-party tracking cookies, screen recording scripts, or behavioral analytics that monitor your keystrokes. Our focus is your productivity, not your personal data profile.
